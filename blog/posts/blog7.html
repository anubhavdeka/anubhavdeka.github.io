loss

10/11/18

loss is a strange feeling, and yet, i have always felt strangely comforted by the idea of loss. i do not know why i felt like this, but it always seemed that the feeling of loss was always present in my life. right from the my earliest memories to now.

i have never been really afraid of losing anyone or anything up until this year. maybe it is because i have changed so much in such a short period, or the fact that i have been never in tune with who i was in the first place itself. or maybe because i lived in a bubble of safety of my own making or maybe because i simply did not care about anything. but i digress. it's such a foreign feeling for me, it's like i have discovered a whole new world that was hidden from my fingertips all this time.

loss. such a short, small word with so much power behind it. power to wage wars, end lives, start new ones, end love and start love as well. form friendships and break blood bonds.

no wonder i was so oblivious to this.

as i probe deeper and deeper into myself as a person, i realize that i tend to hide from my problems, and maybe this is why i always avoided any actions that would let me lose anyone or anything in my life, if you may, i had become a hoarder of sorts. a hoarder of people, secrets and lies. a hoarder of unexpressed feelings and pent up anger. maybe these are the reasons that explain why i am so afraid of opening up in any capacity to anyone in my life.

i was almost certain that i would have lost a person so close to my heart today, that, for the first time in my life, i could feel my eyes welling up. i have now realized, that i have an immensely intense emotional kick when it concerns my family and friends.

maybe this is what the fear of loss is.

maybe this is what gives life meaning.

maybe.
