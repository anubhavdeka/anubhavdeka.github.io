learning

29/11/18

i have only recently realized something about myself that i probably should have known much much earlier.

i love learning.

i don't really care what i am learning about, but as long as the process is ongoing, the high i get off of it is not even close to everything that i have had the opportunity to experiment . this also most probably explains the insanely short attention span that i have and the fact that i like to experiment a lot.

this came to me like a bolt out of the blue in the middle of a class. i realized, that even though i have been clubbing, parties or anything that involves people, i am most at home when i am learning something. the source of the knowledge really doesn't matter, but the fact that i AM learning something, i get this insane high, this indescribable euphoria that i get when i find out that i do not know something. i cannot exactly state why i get this feeling, but, i do. my friends have been incredibly patient with me and me being the textbook definition of a "homebody" (i do like the occasional random ass trip though) and they keep inviting me to parties and stuff and, god bless their little hearts, never take any offense when i eventually refuse.

in other words, i am extremely curious
